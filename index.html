Meet RemaCaffé

RemaCaffé makes approachable European coffee products in Bulgaria, from classic espresso capsules to creamy, café-inspired drinks. EUspresso is bringing the brand to the United States so customers and wholesale partners can enjoy more variety, simple compatibility, and dependable local support.

  • Made in Plovdiv, Bulgaria
  • Roasted, ground, and packaged at origin
  • Produced using renewable solar energy
